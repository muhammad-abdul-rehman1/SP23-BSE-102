overview

intertwined with the growth of the country

The story of Engro is one that has remained intertwined with the growth of Pakistan. We have an undeniable responsibility in our individual and institutional capacity, to solve some of the most pressing issues of our time. In the pursuit of building a better future for Pakistanis, Engro views the challenges faced by the nation as opportunities to change the status quo. We believe our efforts will measurably improve our lives and those of our colleagues, customers, shareholders and the communities in which we live and work.

Engro Corporation (Engro) (Urdu: اینگرو کارپوریشن) is a Pakistani conglomerate headquartered in Karachi.[3][4] Founded as a fertilizer business in 1965, Engro is one of the largest companies in the country,[5] employing over around 3,500 individuals across the Group.[1] It operates in five industry areas: Food and Agriculture, Energy and Related Infrastructure, Petrochemicals, Telecommunication Infrastructure, and International Trading.[5][6]

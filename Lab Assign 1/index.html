Intertwined with the Growth of the Country

The story of Engro is one that has remained intertwined with the growth of Pakistan. We have an undeniable responsibility in our individual and institutional capacity, to solve some of the most pressing issues of our time. In the pursuit of building a better future for Pakistanis, Engro views the challenges faced by the nation as opportunities to change the status quo.

We believe our efforts will measurably improve our lives and those of our colleagues, customers, shareholders and the communities in which we live and work.
